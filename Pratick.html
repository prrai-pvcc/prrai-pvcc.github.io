Interesting Kitten Facts

  • Kittens are born with closed eyes and ears.
  • They can start walking at about 3-4 weeks old.
  • Kittens purr not only when they are happy but also when they are scared or in pain.
  • They are more active during dawn and dusk.
  • A group of kittens is called a "kindle."
